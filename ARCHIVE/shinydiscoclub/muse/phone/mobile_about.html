Shiny Disco Club was founded in Lyon, France in September 2010 with one goal and one only : take part in the rebirth of disco that had already started.

In order to accomplish this mission, the young label called many promising artists of this new scene into one compilation, "Millenium Disco", which reached funky/house and disco fans from all around the world. A few EPs and a year after that, Shiny Disco Club decided to make it happen again and released the second volume of the compilation which knew the exact same success online.

But putting out compilations on the internet isn't the only thing this label does. Many talented artists (such as Cherokee, Kartell or Viceroy) were discovered through the various EPs and singles that were published by the french label. These artists are now recognized worldwide for being ambassadors of both nu disco and French house movements.

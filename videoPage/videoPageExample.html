Charles directs commercials, films and content with a collective of frequent collaborators via his boutique production company  SIMPATICO CONTENT. He is also available as a  FREELANCE DIRECTOR.

MORE INFO.

Working internationally over the past decade alongside agencies such as McCaan’s, M&C Saatchi, Colenso BBDO, Leo Burnett, DDB and Badjar Ogilvy, Charles has directed campaigns for major brands including ANZ, Purex, Ford, Tiger Beer, Honda, Home Timber and Hardware and MyState. He’s also won numerous awards for his work at Tropfest, Telluride, Slamdance, MADC and AWARD among others and his short films have screened at more than 50 international festivals.

Comfortable with documentary subjects, as he is with comedic absurdity, Charles has developed a reputation for detailed, character focused work that is always grounded and human.
